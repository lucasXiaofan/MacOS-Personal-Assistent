# MDAgents: An Adaptive Collaboration of LLMs for Medical Decision-Making

## Paper Information
- **Title**: MDAgents: An Adaptive Collaboration of LLMs for Medical Decision-Making
- **ArXiv URL**: https://arxiv.org/abs/2404.15155
- **Project Page**: https://mdagents2024.github.io/
- **Published**: NeurIPS 2024
- **OpenReview**: https://openreview.net/forum?id=EKdk4vxKO4

## GitHub Repository
- **Repository**: https://github.com/mitmedialab/MDAgents
- **Description**: Official implementation for NeurIPS'24 paper

## Key Information
- A novel **multi-agent framework** that automatically assigns collaboration structures to teams of LLMs
- Tailored to medical tasks - adapts solo or group collaboration based on task complexity
- Incorporates Medical Decision Tables (MDTs) and Interdisciplinary Consultation Teams (ICTs)
- Achieves best performance across diverse medical tasks with optimized efficiency and accuracy
